One strength of your explanation is how you clearly describe the initial loading behavior. For improvement, you might add that CSR can provide smoother dynamic updates after the first load in single-page applications.

Overall, this is a clear and solid comparison that demonstrates a good understanding of the trade-offs between SSR and CSR.
